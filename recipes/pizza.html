Pizza Carnivore Style

This pizza is gonna become your gourmet entry to the carnivore lifestyle!

Ingredients

Crust

  • 250g minced lamb
  • 250g minced beef
  • 1 whole egg
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 100g goat or sheep feta
  • 1 tbsp beef gelatine
  • 1/2 cup sparkling mineral water
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground rosemary

Red Sauce

This is the binding agent for the topping in case you don't want to use too much cheese

  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 1 tsp red paprika powder
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tbsp beef gelatine
  • 50 ml warm water

Topping

  • 150 g Buffalo mozzarella
  • 8 slices smoked prosciutto
  • 6 slices beef jerky
  • 6 slices smoked pancetta

Optional
  • 1 medium pickled green or red hot pepper in slices
  • 1 tbsp grated aged cheese of your choice

Instructions

  1. First, mix the gelatine with sparkling minerl water at room temperature. Now add all the spices to this mixture.
  2. Mix the two kinds of minced meat with egg yolks and one whole egg an then add the gelatine mixture. Crumble the feta cheese with your hands and add it to the meat. Mix it well until it looks like one unit.
  3. Place the meat mixture between 2 parchment papers and roll it out to create a thin crust. It should be approximately 5mm (0.2-inch) thin.
  4. Bake that crust in the overn for 10 minutes at 200°C or 400°F
  5. Prepare the red sauce by mixing warm water with gelatine and 3 egg yolks. Now add red papika powder and sea salt. Sprea this sauceall over the half baked crust.
  6. Cut mozzarella into very thin slices an place in top of the sauce. Top with thin slices of dried meats. You can also add chopped hot peppe and grated aged cheese and oregano on top, too.
  7. Bake for another 10 minutes but turn yuor oven to roasting mode if it has one so that the cheese melts und turns golden brown.
